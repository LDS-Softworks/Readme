## What is "LDS Softworks"?
LDS Softworks LLC is a single-person movement in pro of [FOSS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_and_open-source_software), following the ideals of "Simplicity", "Reliability" and "Non-UNIX" software under the [MIT License](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MIT_License) type.

## Why "LDS"?
LDS Stands for "Lilly's Development Studio", as of this movement was created(and maintained) by [Liliana Aizawa(A.K.A. "Lilly")](https://github.com/jossganerYT156), the head developer of LDS and main reason for such to exist.
